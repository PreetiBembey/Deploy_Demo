FEATURES

Time Saver

Online Learning has given the freedom to study at home at your own convenience. No physical commutation is required. Hence more time is available with the student to focus on their studies.

No Peer Disturbance

With individual Online learning at home, there is no chance of peer disturbance in the class as the Teacher is having control over the Live Class Sessions.


1-2-1 Interaction

With Maths On Phone Online Sessions, students have 1-2-1 interaction with the teacher which helps them to clear their doubt at once.
